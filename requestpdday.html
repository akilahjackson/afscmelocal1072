Professional Development Day FAQs

What's a Professional Development (PD) Day ?

Welcome to AFSCME Local 1072's online Professional Development Day (PDD) request system. This system must be used to request your day. Please make sure to include your phone number and email address so that we may contact you quickly if there are any problems.

Who is Eligible ?

There are a few rules:

  • You must be a member of the Bargaining Unit to be eligible.
  • You must apply 20 CALENDAR days (OR MORE!) ahead of time.
  • No more than 40 employees may take the same day off.
  • This means first come, first served. Popular days usually fill first!

What to Expect

If after one week you do not hear that your Professional Development Day (PDD) has been approved - Follow up with your supervisor to see if they got the approval email.
